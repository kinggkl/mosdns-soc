## Anti-Pollution Verification

Tested GFW-listed domains through mosdns. All results verified against authority sources (SSL certificate, ASN, whois).

| Domain | mosdns IP | ASN / Org | Location | Method Verified | Clean? |
|--------|-----------|-----------|----------|-----------------|:---:|
| facebook.com | 57.145.12.1 | AS32934 Meta | Hong Kong CDN | SSL CN=*.facebook.com, ip-api | ✅ |
| twitter.com | 162.159.140.229 | AS13335 Cloudflare | — | Direct DoH agrees | ✅ |
| youtube.com | 142.250.199.238 | AS15169 Google | US | Direct DoH agrees | ✅ |

### Key Lesson: IP Verification Protocol

**Never infer pollution from unfamiliar IP ranges.** For any suspicious IP, verify:

1. **SSL/TLS Certificate** — `curl -k -v https://IP/` → check CN
2. **ASN / ISP** — `curl ip-api.com/json/IP` → verify organization
3. **Reverse DNS** — `dig -x IP`
4. **HTTP/HTTPS service** — `curl http://IP/` → check response

Example: 57.145.12.1 appeared suspicious but verified as legitimate Facebook Hong Kong CDN node with valid `*.facebook.com` certificate.
